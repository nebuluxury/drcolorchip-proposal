How we'd work together

A partnership, not an invoice.

You've built a brand people trust for over 20 years. I don't want to hand you a $5,000 bill for a project and walk away - I want to help grow this, genuinely, over the next few years. So here's a different arrangement: no project fees, no retainer. We agree on an honest starting point, and then we build the future in this hub - together.

The idea

I'd rather earn it with you.

Charging a flat fee to "do a job" isn't interesting to me here, because I don't think this is a job - I think it's an opportunity. I honestly believe the brand can grow several times over in the next three years. So instead of billing you, I'd rather roll up my sleeves, put my own team and time in, and share in the result. If it works, you win big and I'm thrilled to have been a small part of it. If it doesn't, you're not out a fee.

The whole philosophy in one line: you keep the lion's share of everything we build - and a modest slice of the new growth we create together is what keeps me all-in, bringing you my best every single day. My incentive and yours point in exactly the same direction.
Step one, together

We agree on an honest baseline.

Everything fair starts here. We sit down as partners and friends, look openly at the last few years of sales - whatever window you're comfortable with - and agree on the real trend line. Up 5% a year? Flat? Down a little? Whatever it honestly is, we name it together and write it down. That becomes our shared starting line, and everything gets measured from it. Full transparency, both directions - that's the only way this works.

Who covers what

I invest the work. You fund only the outside pieces.

On me - my investment
  • My time - all of it - at no charge
  • My whole team (dev, design, marketing, AI)
  • Strategy, the builds, the AI integration
  • The latest of everything, from every client I serve
On the company - only when needed
  • Specific outside experts a project truly needs
  • e.g. a logo design contest, a website design contest
  • Advanced tools (e.g. human-sounding AI voice), photography
  • Always small, always transparent, always agreed first
And I'm your filter. Anytime we bring in an outside expert - a photographer, a specialist, anything beyond my team - I personally vet and interview them and manage the work, so you're never buying something blind. On the branding, the site, the automation, the ideas you dream up - I'm right there with you.
What you get

A growth partner, not a vendor.

Skin in the game

Because I only do well when the brand grows above our baseline, I'm not billing hours - I'm chasing results. Your success is literally my paycheck.

Low risk for you

No retainer, no upfront fee for the work itself. If the growth doesn't come, you haven't paid for effort - you've paid for outcomes.

Always current

Every new client I take on teaches me something I bring straight back here. You get the newest, best thinking continuously - I'm motivated to, because it grows us both.

The whole front

DTC and B2B, website and Amazon, email/SMS and AI, brand and outreach - one partner owning the growth picture, not six disconnected vendors.

Total transparency

Open books on the baseline and the results, reviewed together. Partners and friends - decisions made in the open, numbers we both trust.

This isn't a campaign, it's a compounding program. Every report in this hub is a piece of the 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x plan - built patiently, together.
