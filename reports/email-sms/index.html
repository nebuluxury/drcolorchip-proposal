Phase 0

Wake up the 20-year list - carefully.

This list is gold, but a 20-year-old list blasted cold will contain dead addresses and spam traps that can wreck your inbox placement for everyone. We do it the right way:

  • Clean & validate the list, then warm up slowly - start with the most recent, most engaged customers and expand.
  • Re-permission / win-back campaign: a warm "we've missed you, here's what's new + a welcome-back offer" that re-confirms consent. 45% of win-back recipients go on to open future emails.
  • Suppress, don't delete non-responders - protects deliverability while keeping the data.
  • Re-engaging an existing customer costs roughly one-fifth of acquiring a new one. This is your cheapest revenue.
Important: old email sign-ups do not count as consent to text them. SMS requires fresh, explicit opt-in (TCPA) - so we collect phone numbers going forward, we don't text the old list.

The stack

What we'd run it on

Klaviyo is the recommended platform for a store like this - it does email and SMS in one place, has the deepest e-commerce automation and segmentation, and integrates natively with Shopify. Free up to 250 profiles; roughly $100/mo at 5k contacts, ~$400 at 25k, ~$720 at 50k, with SMS billed per-message (~$0.01). Lighter/cheaper alternatives if budget is tight: Shopify Email (10,000 free emails/mo, native) or Omnisend (from $16/mo).
Compliance built in: email follows CAN-SPAM (clear sender, physical address, one-click unsubscribe); SMS follows TCPA (explicit written opt-in, easy STOP). We set both up correctly from day one so the program is bulletproof.
