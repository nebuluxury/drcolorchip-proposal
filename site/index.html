Good questions

Everything you're wondering

How do I find my paint color code?
It's a short code (2-6 characters) usually on a sticker in your driver's door jamb, under the hood, or in the trunk. Or just paste your VIN into the finder above and we'll pull it up for you - no code hunting required.
Will it really match my car?
Yes. Every kit is custom-mixed to your exact factory code and backed by our color-match guarantee. If it doesn't match, we make it right.
What about metallic, pearl, or tri-coat colors?
We match those too. Our on-screen chips even show the metallic sparkle and pearl shift, so you know exactly what you're getting before you order.
I've never done this before - is it hard?
Not at all. The Lite pen-and-spray kit is built for total beginners: find your color, dab it on, blend, done in minutes. Follow the quick video and you're set.
What if I can't find my exact color?
No problem - choose "help me match it" and one of our color experts will personally match it for you. Nobody gets stuck.
How fast do I get it, and what if I'm not happy?
Fast turnaround with free shipping over $50, all backed by our satisfaction and color-match guarantee.
