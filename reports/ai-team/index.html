Report 07 · AI sales & support team

A 24/7 AI team that sounds human.

Right now there's no chat on the site, no one answers the phone after hours, and simple "will it match my car?" questions go unanswered - so those customers buy from a competitor instead. We can build Dr. ColorChip a full AI team - a color concierge, a qualifier, a closer, and a round-the-clock phone rep - that captures those people, answers intelligently, and points them to the exact color they need.

The gap today

Every unanswered visitor is a competitor's sale.

  • No chat. Someone unsure about their color has no way to ask - they leave.
  • No after-hours answer. Evenings and weekends - when people actually work on their cars - the phone rings out.
  • No follow-up. Abandoned carts, unanswered dealer inquiries, and old customers just sit there.
The honest math: even if half of people would rather not talk to an AI, that's still 50% more customers helped than today - because today it's zero. And modern voice AI is good enough that most callers won't mind, or won't even notice.

Industry research is blunt about why this matters: reply to a web lead within 5 minutes and you're up to ~21x more likely to qualify it, and the first business to respond wins the large majority of deals. An AI team responds in seconds, every time, around the clock.

The build

Your AI team.

Not a dumb chatbot - a coordinated set of AI agents, each with a job, trained on Dr. ColorChip's products, colors, and 20 years of how-to knowledge.

Color Concierge

Answers the big question - "will it match my car?" Walks people through finding their code or VIN, explains solid vs. metallic vs. tri-coat, and lands them on the exact color and kit.

The Qualifier

Figures out what they actually need - a chip vs. a scratch, one car vs. a fleet, retail vs. dealer - and routes them to the right kit, add-ons, or the dealer program.

The Closer

Handles hesitation ("is it hard?", "what if it doesn't match?"), reassures with the guarantee, recommends the right kit + accessories, and gets the order over the line.

The Front Desk (voice)

Answers every phone call 24/7 in a natural, human-sounding voice - handles questions, takes orders, and cleanly hands off to a person when it should.

The Outreach Rep

Outbound follow-up by voice and text - recovers abandoned carts, chases dealer leads, and reactivates old customers with a friendly, on-brand nudge.

Marketplace & email desk

Drafts fast answers to Amazon Q&A, product questions, and support email so nothing sits for days - keeping ratings and response times high.

On the "I don't like AI" worry

Good enough that most won't mind.

  • Natural voice & language. Today's AI voices are conversational, not robotic - it listens, understands context, and responds like a knowledgeable person.
  • Honest & graceful. It's transparent when asked, and the moment something needs a human it hands off cleanly with full context - no "start over."
  • Trained on your expertise. Two decades of how-to content becomes its knowledge base, so the answers are genuinely helpful and correct.
What it takes

The investment

A full AI team like this is a one-time build (agents, training on your catalog + content, voice setup, and wiring it into your site, phone, and store) plus a modest monthly for hosting and per-minute/per-message usage. Comparable full "AI-team" builds run in the mid four figures to build; we scope yours to exactly what you want to turn on first and grow from there.

Start small, prove it, scale. We'd typically launch the website Color Concierge + chat first (cheapest, immediate), add after-hours voice next, then outbound follow-up. Every stage pays for itself before the next one turns on.
