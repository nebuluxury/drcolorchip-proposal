Report 04 · Product clarity

One system. A few simple steps. Pick how far you go.

The single hardest thing for a new customer is realizing how easy this is. Every kit uses the same factory-matched paint and the same blend step - the only thing that changes is how many tools you get and how big a job you can tackle. Here's the whole thing on one page.

It's easier than it looks

The whole system in 3 steps.

1

Match

Find your car's exact factory color by code or VIN. Your paint is custom-mixed to match - no guessing, no paint-code hunting.

2

Apply

Brush the paint right into the chip, or use the Squirt & Squeegee for bigger scratches. Takes minutes, no experience needed.

3

Blend

The SealAct blending solution wipes away the excess and feathers the edges into your paint - the step that makes it near-invisible. This is the magic.

The one thing to remember: every kit does these same 3 steps with the same paint. More expensive kits just add better applicator tools (mini-squeegee, pipettes, full squeegee) and more product - so you can fix bigger areas, more spots, or a second car. Learn it once; pick the size that fits your job.
